Quick Notes

This app uses the 78 Tarot cards with images from the Rider-Waite deck. To enable reversals or choose a different spread, click on the button in the bottom right corner.

The three-card spread represents the past, present, and future. The five-card spread represents the past, recent past, present, near future, and future. The seven-card spread represents the past, present, hidden influences, yourself, the influence of others, what you should do, and the outcome.

When formulating your question, avoid the ones that can only be answered with yes or no. For example, instead of asking, "Will I get married next year," you should ask, "What do I need to do to get married next year."
